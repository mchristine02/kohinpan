Our Story

Discover the passion behind Kohi N Pan

How We Started

Kōhī n Pan was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality coffee and freshly baked bread to our community. What started as a small corner shop has grown into a beloved local institution.

Our founder, Chef Tanaka, combined his Japanese heritage with his passion for artisanal baking to create a unique culinary experience that blends Eastern and Western traditions.

Our Philosophy

At Kōhī n Pan, we believe that quality ingredients make all the difference. We source our coffee beans from sustainable farms around the world and use only the finest organic flour and ingredients in our baked goods.

We're committed to craftsmanship, taking the time to perfect each recipe and prepare everything with care and attention to detail.

Our Team

Our team of skilled baristas and bakers are passionate about their craft. Many have trained internationally and bring diverse culinary perspectives to our offerings.

We're like a family, working together to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere for our customers and to consistently deliver products we're proud of.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices in everything we do.

Community

We believe in giving back and supporting the community that supports us.

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our ingredients or products.

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our business practices.
